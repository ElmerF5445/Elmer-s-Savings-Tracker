Change wallpaper

Due to browser restriction and safety reasons, the launcher is not allowed to get the direct path of the image in your disk. Instead, you have to put the image you want as your wallpaper in Assets -> Background in the launcher's folder.
Below, specify the file name and file extension of the image. Click the "Detect" button to check if the launcher is able to detect the image through the image preview below. Once you're done, click "Done".
Leave the input field blank if you don't want a wallpaper applied (The background color will be the general color).
